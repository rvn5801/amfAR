About the EHE Initiative

The Ending the HIV Epidemic (EHE) initiative, launched in 2019, aims to reduce new HIV infections in the U.S. by 90% by 2030. It focuses on four pillars: Diagnose, Treat, Prevent, and Respond. The baseline year is 2017.
